Bolognaise

Description

This traditional Italian dish is a true crowd pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 500g minced meat
  • 1 onions
  • 1 carrot
  • 200 ml redwine
  • 500 ml broth

Steps

  1. Fry the onion in the pan
  2. Add the carrot
  3. Add the minced meat
  4. Add the redwine
  5. Cook for many hours
