Züri-Gschnätzlets

Description

This delicious dish is one of the most satisfying dishes in Zurich.

Ingredients

  • 500g young beef
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 1 onion
  • 200g chamignon
  • 1 dl white wine

Steps

  1. Fry the onion in the pan
  2. Add the beef
  3. Add the white wine
  4. add the champignong
  5. Cook for 30 minutes
