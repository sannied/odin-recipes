Hummus

Description

This hummus recipe only takes 10 minutes to prepare and will be popular at any picnic.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of chickpeas
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp Tahini
  • paprika

Steps

  1. Mix the tahini with lemon juice
  2. Add olive oil and mix again
  3. Add the chickpeas
  4. Mix everything togher
  5. Add more olive oil, salt, and paprika
